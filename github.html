COMMUNITY AND SAFETY

Community and Safety (or CampS for short), is a team within Github that focuses on closing abuse vectors on Github and building tools to build a more inclusive environment at Github. I got to work on a variety of small projects and 1 large project. I learned a lot about research, writing documentation, and designing with product philosophy in mind.

MAIN LEARNINGS

I learned a lot about community and safety from being on the team. Here are some of my main learnings:

  • 1. Any feature can be abused. As designers, it is our job to ensure that we are aware of the ways that people can abuse features and provide reporting and blocking mechanisms.
  • 2. Presentation and design critique skills are important. Know what you want feedback on and how to present that work.
  • 3. A prototype is much more effective than a mockup.
