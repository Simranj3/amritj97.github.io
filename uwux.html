UW/UX

School Club, Summer 2017 - Present

I've been developing UW/UX, the only User Experience Design club at the University of Waterloo. I've worn multiple hats at this club, starting from being a mentee, to leading a group of 4-5 passionate designers to reach our long term vision.

INTRODUCTION

UW/UX aims to build a community of designers at the University of Waterloo through teaching people about design, holding workshops and faciliating collaboration amongst students. I've developed a bootcamp-like syllabus for Fall 2018 and am excited to deliver the lectures with a group of students.


BRANDING

I led a mini re-brand of the club after realizing we needed to have more creative freedom for our media assets and that we had an opportunity to grow.
